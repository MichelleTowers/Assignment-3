Mobile Developer

About us

Started in 2022, A group of young passionate people about IT who wanted to create something and did. They all met whilst studying at Uni and became close friends and decided to collaborate as business partners.

What is Traqstarz?

Traqstarz is an Android-based application aimed at people who enjoy motorbikes and going to tracks, our app uses GPS tracking and telemetry data gathering to show statistics of a user's session while they are at a racetrack, our features such as map view, map tracking, personal statistics including top speeds, track completions and lap times.

Our Mission?

To offer a more modern, cleaner GUI with a more user-friendly layout than the existing applications, we want to create a safe community for like minded people who have a passion for racing and make sure they have fun doing it!

About you

We are looking for a Mobile Developer to Design, develop and implement mobile applications. Our ideal candidate will have 3-5 years of experience, working in a boutique or start up environment will be highly beneficial.

Responsibilities

- Development of Android software development kits
- Troubleshoot issues for mobile devices
- Liaise with business partners to understand app requirement specifications
- Good knowledge of Android Design patterns and coding conventions

Skills needed

- Demonstrable real-world React Native experience with a project that has been publicly released
- Experience with other mobile development frameworks (Cordova / Swift / Ionic / Xamarin)
- Experience across HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, as well as server-side development
- Unit testing experience using Jest / Mocha / Enzyme
- Understanding what Component driven design means

Qualifications

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent degree

Benefits

- Competitive Salary
- Flexible working arrangements
- Supportive and diverse team
- Career Progression
